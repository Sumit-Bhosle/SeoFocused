About Us

Serving Since 1950

Our Story

A legacy brewed with passion and love for over 70 years.

KOPPEE began as a humble café in the heart of Bangalore in 1950. With a commitment to serving freshly brewed organic coffee, we quickly became a neighborhood favorite. From cozy corners for conversations to takeaway cups for busy mornings, every sip tells a story of tradition, warmth, and community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a space where every coffee lover feels at home. We believe in sustainability, supporting local farmers, and delivering quality with every cup. Whether it's your daily dose of caffeine or a weekend hangout, KOPPEE is here to serve experiences, not just coffee.
